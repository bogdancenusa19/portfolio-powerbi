The primary focus of this dashboard is to answer a few specific business questions raised by the client, using fictional or embellished datasets tailored to meet educational goals.

Project Summary

  • Tools used: Power BI, Power Query
  • Data source: Simulated sales & marketing data from a retail business
  • Pages: Campaign Performance, Buyer Composition, Purchase Drivers
  • Main KPIs: Sales per Product Category, Purchases per Campaign, Customer Count, Average Income, Discounts Purchased, and Purchase Channel Distribution

Questions

  • 1. How are our 6 recent marketing campaigns performing?
  • 2. How are our products performing?
  • 3. Who are our customers?
  • 4. What is driving campaign performance and buyer decision-making?
