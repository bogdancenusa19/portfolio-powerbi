Some DAX Measures

Total Votes

Total Votes = SUM(Votes[Votes])

Total Candidates

Total Candidates = COUNT(Candidates[Candidate_id])

Total Votes per County

Total Votes per County = CALCULATE([Total Votes], ALLEXCEPT(Votes, County[County_id]))

Total Votes per Candidate

Total Votes per Candidate = CALCULATE([Total Votes], ALLEXCEPT(Votes, Candidates[Candidate_id]))

Average Report Hour of Votes

Average Report Hour of Votes = AVERAGE(Votes[Submission Hour])

Candidate Rank

Candidate Rank = RANKX(ALL(Candidates[Candidate_id]), [Total Votes],, DESC, Dense)

These measures leverage DAX techniques including CALCULATE, FILTER and RANKX to generate dynamic and actionable insights.
